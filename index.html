INSIGHT

Our company wants to help in the improvement of the world, and what better way to do it then by helping to achieve one of the ONU 17 goals. Our team decided to help with goal number 7 which is Affordable and Clean Energy. The product we made hits both marks and we wish to show our invention to the world.

Learn more about us

Our product is based from the Faraday law of induction. This makes it possible to transform kinetic energy into magnetic energy and then to electric energy. By this and help from a rectifier, condenser, voltage regulator, It’s able to charge a phone. The alternator in the bike generates alternate energy and by moving the pedal, it begins to generate voltage.

Because it’s alternate, it is needed to make it continous and lower the voltages also making them stables. With the before mentioned helpful tools, The circuit’s ready. All left is to connect it to the bike and start pedaling.
